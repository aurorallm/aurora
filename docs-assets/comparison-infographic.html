Edition Comparison

Aurora Gateway

Open-source AI gateway for everyone, from solo builders shipping their first integrations to platform teams standardizing routing, caching, governance, observability, and cost control across large-scale AI deployments.

Open Source

Aurora OSS

Price: Free — Apache 2.0 License
Core AI gateway with broad provider coverage, OpenAI-compatible and Anthropic-compatible ingress, semantic caching, guardrails, audit logging, usage analytics, and an embedded admin dashboard. Ideal for local development, internal tooling, and self-hosted production rollouts.
For developers & small teams
Enterprise

Aurora Enterprise

Price: Custom — Signed commercial license
Everything in OSS plus SSO, RBAC, multi-tenant isolation, spend budgets, HA clustering, compliance controls, observability exports, adaptive routing, advanced guardrails, and priority support. Built for organizations that need governance, stronger security boundaries, and predictable controls at scale.
For organizations at scale
